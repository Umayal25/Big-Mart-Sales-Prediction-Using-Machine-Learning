Welcome to Bigmart Sales Prediction

The Bigmart Sales Prediction project helps store owners predict their sales based on various parameters. By analyzing historical data, owners can make informed decisions to optimize inventory, pricing, and promotions, leading to increased sales and profitability.

Owners can input specific details such as item weight, visibility, type, maximum retail price, outlet size, location type, and more to generate accurate sales predictions. This web application simplifies the prediction process and empowers store owners to make data-driven decisions.

Explore the predictive power of our tool by clicking on the predict your sales button!

These are the parameters for which you need to feed your values,and the description of it is given below for your reference!

Attributes Description
Item_Identifier Unique product ID
Item_Weight Weight of product
Item_Fat_Content Whether the product is low fat or not
Item_Visibility The % of total display area of all products in a store allocated to the particular product
Item_Type The category to which the product belongs
Item_MRP Maximum Retail Price (list price) of the product
Outlet_Identifier Unique store ID
Outlet_Establishment_Year The year in which store was established
Outlet_Size The size of the store in terms of ground area covered
Outlet_Location_Type The type of city in which the store is located
Outlet_Type Whether the outlet is just a grocery store or some sort of supermarket
